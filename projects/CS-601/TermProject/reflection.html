Reflection

A rewarding experience

Experience

I never thought that building an e-portfolio would be this rewarding. I took this class with the goal of adding a plus to my resume that will help me out with my job search. Since the field of information technology is getting competitive every day, it’s not enough with just showing your resume, you need to show more of you to be ahead of your competition and with an e-portfolio it would be a good tool showcase my skills. Yet the process to building it hasn’t been an easy one.

Process

The process of selecting the artifacts wasn’t easy at all. While selecting the artifacts, it became clear that I wasn’t looking at them critically enough. I was focused only on one part of them without noticing other details that will help me achieve a balance between them. With this new point of view, I was able to categorize the items by skills. For example I can choose certain examples from previous job experiences that will highlight skills in Object Oriented programming and so on with other examples. Being able to complete the selection of artifacts, it made evaluate my professional career in another way it made me see the strengths and weaknesses I need to work on.

At last

As I put the last pieces of this portfolio together, this experience has been very rewarding. It gave new perspective on how to evaluate my career, as my professional career grows in the Information Technology field. As I achieve new goals through my career, I will keep adding them to my “professional museum” that is this e-portfolio.
