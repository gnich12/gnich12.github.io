Description

For this project, I help my client with her online portafolio as a requirement for her job interviews. i recomended her wordpress instead of a static website since it would be easy for her to post more content as need it, and also with the variety of template available online that would easily satisfy her requirements.

Development

As for the design for her website, after going through many templates online we decided to go with the oxygen template. This template has a look and feel of a magazine that will help showcase all the media content. you can take a look at the oxygen template After, we concluded that we wanted to move forward with this template, i modified some of the color to purple of the lakers. To achive this i use one of the Firefox developers tools Firebug with that i was able to modified the CSS
